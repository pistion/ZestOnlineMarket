FAQ

Buying & Shipping Art

Are these original artworks or prints? +
You can sell both on this template. Each product card should clearly state if it is an original, limited edition print or open print.
Do you ship outside PNG? +
Yes, this layout is designed for PNG-based artists who want to ship regionally and internationally. Your real store settings will control which countries you support.
How are artworks packed? +
We recommend rigid packaging, corner protectors and waterproof wrapping. Use the description area on each artwork to explain your packing process.
Can I request a custom piece? +
Yes. The “Request Commission” button at the top can be wired to your real commission form or chat flow in production.
