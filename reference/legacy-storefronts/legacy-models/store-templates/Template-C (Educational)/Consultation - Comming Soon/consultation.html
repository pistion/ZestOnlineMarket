About this work

This is a space for honest conversations, precise language around what you’re carrying, and practical strategy for what comes next.

My work sits at the intersection of psychology, strategy and story. Clients often come in carrying invisible pressure — growing companies, caring for families, holding communities together — but without a space that is purely theirs.

Together we map what’s happening in your nervous system, your relationships and your calendar. Then we rebuild your internal and external systems so that your life, work and body are moving in the same direction.

You can expect gentle challenge, clear language, and a pace that honours both your ambition and your humanity.
